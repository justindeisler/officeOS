# Social Content Creator

Build your professional brand and attract freelance clients through strategic social content.

## Platform Strategies

### LinkedIn
**Audience:** Decision makers, hiring managers, potential clients
**Tone:** Professional but personable
**Best content:**
- Project case studies
- Technical insights (accessible)
- Industry observations
- Career/business lessons learned

**Optimal format:**
- 1,200-1,500 characters
- Line breaks for readability
- Hook in first 2 lines (before "see more")
- End with question or CTA

### Twitter/X
**Audience:** Tech community, developers, startup founders
**Tone:** Conversational, authentic
**Best content:**
- Quick tips and insights
- Real-time learnings
- Thread deep-dives
- Engagement with community

**Optimal format:**
- Single tweets: 200-280 characters
- Threads: 5-12 tweets
- Use numbers and lists
- Visual content when possible

## Content Calendar Ideas

### Weekly Themes
- **Monday:** Motivation/week goals
- **Tuesday:** Technical tips
- **Wednesday:** Behind the scenes
- **Thursday:** Industry insights
- **Friday:** Wins/lessons from the week

### Monthly Goals
- 2-3 project showcases
- 4-5 technical insights
- 3-4 engagement posts
- 1-2 personal/story posts

## Writing Tips

### Hooks That Work
- Numbers: "5 things I learned..."
- Controversy: "Unpopular opinion:..."
- Story: "Last week, a client asked..."
- Problem: "Struggling with...?"
- Result: "How we increased X by Y%"

### Formatting
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences)
- Use line breaks liberally
- Emojis as visual markers (not excessive)
- Lists and bullets
- Bold key points (LinkedIn)

### CTAs
- "What's your experience?"
- "Save this for later"
- "DMs open for questions"
- "Link in comments"
- "Follow for more [topic]"

## Don't
- Be overly salesy
- Only post about yourself
- Use corporate jargon
- Post without engaging with others
- Ignore comments on your posts
- Post inconsistently

## Hashtag Strategy

**LinkedIn:** 3-5 relevant hashtags at the end
- #FullStackDeveloper
- #WebDevelopment
- #FreelanceDeveloper
- #TechConsulting
- #[Technology specific]

**Twitter:** 1-2 hashtags max, integrated naturally
